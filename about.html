About us

MegaBath, INC. is a leading sanitary ware manufacturer and exporter in China. Our products are ISO9001 certified.

Since 1999, we have made and exported a wide range of bathroom accessories including basket, towel bar & ring, robe hook, hair dryer holder, tumbler holder, soap dispenser, and toilet paper holder/ brush.

We provide products made from stainless steel to suit daily use as well as high end products made of brass to let you enjoy the beauty of life.

The innovative design and high quality have made our products popular in Europe (especially German and Spain), East Asia, and also in China mainland. We have exported in large quantities to more than 80 countries all around the world every year.

We appreciate every business opportunity and look forward to providing you high quality products and services.
